Meet our clients

Our members have worked with the BBC, Mozilla, Greenpeace, Moodle, Creative Commons and tens of other household names. As a co-op, our clients benefit from the multitude of skills and expertise our members possess.

In the early days of the Creative Commons Certificate program, we met with the team to run a think-a-thon. Over the years, the We Are Open Co-op has done a variety of things with CC. We're huge fans of their work!
